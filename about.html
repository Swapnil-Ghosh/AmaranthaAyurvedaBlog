About Us

Set up in January 2002 by Dr. Anisha and Sanjeevan Kanjilal, Ari Healthcare Pvt Ltd. (AHPL) started with managing institutional sales of pharmaceutical companies and since then has grown and diversified to be a strong contender in the pharma world. After over a decade of domestic sales and export activities in the allopathic space, we decided to channel our expertise into developing and formulating our own products. Ari Healthcare Pvt Ltd. now has a renewed sharp focus on Nature Based Products. Our own strong belief in the efficacy of these products and a statistically backed global shift towards their usage has encouraged us to set up our own R&D centre at Hinjewadi’s Biotech Park, Pune. These products are subjected to clinical trials to further prove their efficacy. We have access to world-class manufacturing facilities with state-of-the-art technology. We incorporate best practices and adhere to stringent regulatory compliances. Plants are built as per WHO-GMP guidelines. With an uncompromising commitment to quality, Ari Healthcare’s promise to its customers is that every product will meet and surpass the highest of standards.
