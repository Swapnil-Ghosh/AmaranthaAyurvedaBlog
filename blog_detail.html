Fight Acne Inside and Outside - The Ayurvedic Way

08 May 2017 Blog

Acne has always been a tormenting experience, be it small red bumps on the skin, or painful cysts filled with pus. It’s definitely a confidence killer and can keep you from looking or feeling your best! If you’re currently dealing with acne, then this one’s for you.

The first step in your fight against acne, is patience. In a world where instant gratification is the norm, the struggle is real when the same can’t be applied to medical ailments! The two ways to fight acne, are’ inside out’ and ‘outside in’, both taking place simultaneously.

Using medicinal/cosmetic face creams are merely a temporary solution to get rid of troublesome acne. To fight it permanently, it has to be done straight from the root.

What causes acne?


● Hormonal imbalances

● Excessive Androgen

● Activation of Sebaceous glands during puberty

● Stress

● Lack of sleep, and

● Poor eating habits

When you fight acne from inside out, the right medicine helps open blocked pores and balances hormone levels , making acne mild and eventually curing it completely. This will further prevent break outs, as the root cause of acne has been eliminated. Moving on to the curing process of outside in, the face creams help get rid of scars, inflammation, and redness. They help dry the skin and eventually work simultaneously with clearing the skin from within.

Why go Ayurveda?

Undoubtedly, allopathic skin remedies have been extremely successful in treating acne. But they do have their side effects, which can be long lasting, and hamper the process of skin treatment. According to Healthline Magazine, some acne treatments can have severe side effects like nausea, aggression, changes in menstrual cycle and chest pain.

This is a high cost to pay!

Here’s where Ayurvedic medicine comes into the picture.

According to Ayurveda, skin type depends on the Doshas or energies within people – Vata, Pitta &Kapha. Vata can be associated with dry skin, Pitta with sensitive skin and Kapha with oily skin. Even though traditional Ayurveda consisted of medicinal treatment for each of these doshas, today, Ayurvedic medicine can heal every skin type using natural herbs such as Neem, Sariva, Khair & many others, which are known to be useful, after years of research.

These natural remedies do take time to work, as each ailment is treated with the right medication and eventually gets cleared out from the system completely. This is then followed by the Ayurvedic treatment of outside in , where the goodness of natural herbs are then used to clear blemishes, scars and helps uplift the skin.

Unfortunately, a lot of Ayurvedic medicines lack research, the technology or even the correct ingredients in the right quantities to create good Ayurvedic remedies. The trick is therefore to find and use products that have been researched, formulated and use standardised extracts. It is this research, development and formulation that plays an important role in ensuring such medicine does not cause any side effects.
